FAQs

Profile

Change your profile details

Go to your profile. There your can go through "change profile" to a page where you can update your profile.

Recharge credit

On your profile you can recharge credit bei adding an amount and your iban

Transactions

On your profile you can go to an overview of all transactions. You also see on your profile you actual amount and blocked cautions.

Products

add new Product

On the sidenav you can chose add Product to create a new Product. After that you can upload an image or use the default image.

Search Products

At the page search Products you can search specific products, have an overview of your lend products and about borrowed products.

Edit Product

Chose your product at the products search. After that you have the option to edit your Product. There you can also delete this product, if its actual not borrowed

Borrow a product

1. Search a product, go to details and chose borrow it
2. Now you can add a comment to the owner and chose the period you want to borrow it
3. In the requests section you have an overview over all orderProcesses
4. The owner of the product can now accept or deny your request.
5. After you return the product, the owner can accept or deny your return and the dailyFee will be paid from your account.


Deny a return

You are not confirm with the return of a product? If you chose deny return, an admin will get a notification about it and will check this orderProcess. If he confirms the conflict, the caution will be paid automatically.

Requests

Have a look at your requests! You can not only see your borrowed products there, you have also to check requests that are send to yourself. Accept or deny orderProcesses and have an overview of returned products.
